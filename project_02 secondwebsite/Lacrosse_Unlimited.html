Lacrosse Unlimited
GRAPHIC DESIGN / APPAREL DESIGN (2019 - PRESENT)
Founded in 1990 by the DeSimone family, Lacrosse Unlimited was the first sports store to specialize solely in Lacrosse on Long Island. The first store was located in Hauppauge, NY. From the Success expanded the brand to lacrosse hotbeds nation-wide. Lacrosse Unlimited currently has 44 stores in 12 states.

I transformed client requests into visual representations for proofs and production while maintaining the standards set forth by the Graphic Design Team Manager. I would Illustrate concepts and ideas for client approval and correspond with in-house team to efficiently relay ideas into final approved designs.
