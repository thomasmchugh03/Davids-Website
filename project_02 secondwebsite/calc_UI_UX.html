Motion / Visual Graphic (2018)

To show tribute to an aspiring and talented poet, New Opera Live Series, The Performing Arts Center at Purchase College, presented an innovative and remarkable variations of Nayyirah Waheed’s poems. For this exhibition, I composed a 24x36 inch poster that communicates Waheed’s arousing and controversial poem “The Box Circle”. “The Box Circle” explores an array of ideas, including the lesson of learning and growing from your mistakes.
